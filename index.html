My thoughts

I'm a born traveler. By the time I turned 18 I'd been to over 26 countries on every continent except Africa. I was born in Taipei in 1990 when my parents moved there for business.

What fascinates me about visiting other countries is that it broadens your horizons. It opens your mind up to new ways of thinking and living and exposes you to things you don't normally engage with.
